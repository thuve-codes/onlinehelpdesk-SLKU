Sri Lanka Knowledge University

The prestigious Sri Lanka Knowledge University (SLKU) is committed to promoting excellence in education, research, and innovation. SLKU takes pride in its dedication to both academic excellence and societal growth, having been founded with the goal of being a premier centre for knowledge generation and dissemination in Sri Lanka. At SLKU, we emphasise developing our students' intellectual curiosity since it helps them develop into critical thinkers, problem solvers, and future leaders in their areas.

Students, professors, lecturers, instructors and staff at our university are a diverse and brilliant group committed to creating a better future for Sri Lanka and beyond. SLKU aspires to meaningfully contribute to society through significant community engagement programmes, innovative studies, and creative teaching approaches. Being a comprehensive university, SLKU makes sure that its students obtain a well-rounded education that equips them for the challenges of the future by providing a wide choice of undergraduate, graduate, and doctorate programmes across numerous fields. SLKU offers the field of studies in IT, business, engineering and humanities. SLKU offers the ideal setting for you to succeed.

SLKU Library

The library of Sri Lanka Knowledge University (SLKU) serves as a vital hub for academic resources and research support, embodying the institution's commitment to fostering knowledge and innovation. It offers a comprehensive collection of books, journals, and digital resources covering diverse subjects such as IT, business, engineering, and humanities, aligning with SLKU's multidisciplinary approach to education. The library provides a conducive environment for study and research, equipped with modern facilities and technologies to facilitate learning. Additionally, it offers services such as reference assistance, interlibrary loan, and digital access to scholarly databases, enhancing the academic experience for students, faculty, and researchers. Through its resources and services, the SLKU library plays a pivotal role in supporting the university's mission of academic excellence and knowledge dissemination.

SLKU Shuttle Serive

The shuttle service at Sri Lanka Knowledge University provides a convenient and efficient transportation option for students, faculty, and staff. With a focus on ensuring safe and timely travel, the shuttle service operates on a regular schedule, serving key locations within and around the university campus. This service not only helps reduce traffic congestion but also promotes sustainability by encouraging the use of public transportation. The university's shuttle service is known for its reliability and accessibility, catering to the diverse needs of the university community. Whether commuting to classes, attending events, or accessing off-campus facilities, the shuttle service plays a vital role in enhancing the overall campus experience, fostering a sense of community, and facilitating seamless mobility for all members of the university.
