Career Guidence and Services

Vision of careerguidance unit of SLKU is to become center of excellemce in ensuring the employability of all the graduates and who completer their studies at the university. Students of SLKU are made aware of the employment opportunities availabe inside and outside the country by the CGU. CGU guides every person individually to make teir future brighter through gaining every indivuals performances. CGU creates a bridge between undergraduates ans the public and public sectors.

Undergraduates

Career Guidance Unit helps undergraduates to develop their skills,abilities,personality and enhances the understanding of career options.Success in career guidance depends on providing upto date information on training & employment oppotunities and having a good knowledge of available opportunities in the training market.

Employers

We conduct many team activities with software companies so we can merge with new employers and display our own talent among people. We share the opportunities and vacancies for jobs among each other so you can choose a job as per your requirements.

Career guidance events in campus offer valuable opportunities to explore the potential of induviduals. Cultural Events

Pongal Celebration

Pongal celebration is a colorful event that honours the traditional harvest festival. Students and faculty unites together and offer sweets like sweet pongal and savory dishes among each other. Every students comes with traditional dresses and make the campus environment colourful. Activities includes decorating the campus, showcasing traditional music ,dance and drama.

Tamil Sinhala New Year

Tamil and Sinhala New Year celebration demonstrate the cultural significance of the occasion. Festival includes traditional rituals,music,dance and performance.Its the event where everyone can come together to celebrate and embrace the culture.

Iftar Celebration

Iftar celebration unites the students in observing the breaking of fast duirng Ramadan. Through this celebration, the universityresffirms its commitment to diversity, inclusion and respect for religious traditions,creating an environment where all can join together.

Christmas Celebration

Christmas celebration decorations adorns the campus, creating a festive ambiance filled with lightings around the Christmas tree. various activities such as carol singing, holiday concerts and performance of students highlights the stage. Christmas celebrationspreads goodwill throughout the campus.

Freshers Day

Freshers day in campus is an most anticipated event that welcome new students to the university community. The day is filled with a variety of activities and programmes, including orientation sessions, campus tours and cultural performances. Senior students provide guidanceand support to the new students academic journey. This day is a summary of every students academic journey in a common perspective.
